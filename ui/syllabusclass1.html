Syllabus for Class 1 Maths

Class 1 Syllabus makes the first stage of schooling easy. From here, the journey of a student starts. At this stage, students have a very sharp mind and quick learning ability. They are easily mouldable and can adapt to any environment. So, in these formative years class 1 students should be provided with quality education,have a look at latest class 1 maths syllabus given below

Students can have a look at the topics of Class 1 Maths Syllabus below>

  1. Geometry
    • Shapes and Spatial Understanding
    • Solids around us
  2. Numbers
    • Developing a sense of Numberness, Counting and Operations of Numbers 1-9 and Zero
    • Numbers from (10 – 20)
    • Addition And Subtraction (Upto 20)
    • Numbers from 21 – 99
    • Mental Arithmetic
  3. Measurement
    • Length
    • weight
    • Patterns
