Syllabus for Class 2 Maths

Class 2 syllabus is framed in an interactive manner in form of short stories and poems with some kind of moral lessons in order to develop their in-depth understanding of the concepts.

Students can have a look at the topics of Class 2 Maths Syllabus below>

  1. Geometry
    • Shapes and Spatial Understanding
    • Solids around us
  2. Numbers
    • Addition and Subtraction
    • Multiplication and Division
    • Mental arithmetic
    • Money
  3. Measurement
    • Length
    • Weight
    • Patterns
    • Capacity
    • Time
  4. Data Handling

Syllabus for Class 2 EVS

EVS or environmental studies is one of the 3 major subjects that you learn in class 2. Maths and English being the other 2, students usually give less importance to EVS. But, as a surprise to many, environmental science is as important as the rest as it delivers the important etiquettes that have to be adopted by the students in real life.

Students can have a look at the topics of Class 2 EVS Syllabus below

  1. Others in my World
  2. My Needs – Food
  3. My Needs – Water
  4. My Need – Shelter
  5. My Need – Clothing
  6. My Need – Air
  7. Keeping oneself Clean, Safe and Healthy
  8. Places in the Neighbourhood
  9. Plants
  10. Animals
  11. Transport
  12. Communication
  13. The World Around Me: Sun, Moon, Sky and Stars
  14. Time, Space, Direction
